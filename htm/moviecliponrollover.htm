MovieClip.onRollOver

myMovieClip.onRollOver

 

Where myMovieClip is a movie clip instance.

 

Compatible with Flash 6 and later.

 

The MovieClip.onRollOverevent handler is invoked when the mouse moves over a movie clip area.

 

This event is the same as the corresponding Button event Button.onRollOver. See the entry for Button.onRollOver.
