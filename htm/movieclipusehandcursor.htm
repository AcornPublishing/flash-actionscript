MovieClip.useHandCursor

myMovieClip._useHandCursor

 

Where myMovieClip is a movie clip instance.

 

Compatible with Flash 6 and later.

 

The MovieClip._useHandCursorproperty indicates whether to use a hand cursor when over a button movie clip.

 

This event is the same as the corresponding Button event Button.useHandCursor. See the entry for Button.useHandCursor.
