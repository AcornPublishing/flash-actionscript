MovieClip.trackAsMenu

myMovieClip.trackAsMenu

 

Where myMovieClip is a movie clip instance.

 

Compatible with Flash 4 and later.

 

The MovieClip.trackAsMenu property is a Boolean indicating whether buttons and movie clips can receive mouse events.

 

This event is the same as the corresponding Button event Button.trackAsMenu. See the entry for Button.trackAsMenu.
