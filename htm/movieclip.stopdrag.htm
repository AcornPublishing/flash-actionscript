MovieClip.stopDrag

MyMovieClip.stopDrag()

 

Where myMovieClip is a movie clip instance.

 

Compatible with Flash 5 and later.

 

The MovieClip.stopDrag()method is used to stop a movie clip from being draggable.

Description

The MovieClip.stopDrag()method turns off the draggability of a movie clip that was made draggable by a MovieClip.startDrag()method.

 

 

Code

 

 

Additional explanation

 

 

Notes

 

 

myMovieClip.stopDrag();

 

 

Stops myMovieClip from being draggable.

 

Only one movie clip is draggable at a time.

 

 

stopDrag(myMovieClip);

 

 

No parameters are allowed.

 

 

 

Examples and practical uses

The MovieClip.stopDrag()method lets you stop a movie clip from being draggable:

 

myMovieClip.stopDrag();

 

See MovieClip.startDrag() for more information on dragging clips.

Tips and precautions

The MovieClip.stopDrag()and MovieClip.startDrag()methods allow you to turn draggability on and off as necessary.
