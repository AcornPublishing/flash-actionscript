Math.E

a = Math.E;

 

Where a is the variable that you want to equate to the mathematical constant e.

 

This method is compatible with Flash 5 or later.

Description

This constant is the base of all natural logarithms, and is expressed as e in mathematics. It is approximately equal to 2.71828182845905.

 

 

Code

 

 

Notes

 

a = Math.E;

 

b = Math.log(Math.E);

 

 

In the first line, a will be set to approximately 2.718.

 

In the second line, b will be set to 1, which is the result of taking the natural logarithm of e. See Math.log

 

Examples and practical uses

None

Tips and precautions

In Flash 4, the methods of the Math object are emulated, and may therefore not always be as accurate as in Flash 5 and above.
