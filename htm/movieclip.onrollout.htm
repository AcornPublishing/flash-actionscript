MovieClip.onRollOut

myMovieClip.onRollOut

 

Where myMovieClip is a movie clip instance.

 

Compatible with Flash 6 and later.

 

The MovieClip.onRollOutevent handler is invoked when the mouse moves outside a movie clip area.

 

This event is the same as the corresponding Button event Button.onRollOut. See the entry for Button.onRollOut.
