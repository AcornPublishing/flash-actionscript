MovieClip.unloadMovie

myMovieClip.unloadMovie()

 

Where myMovieClip is a movie clip instance.

 

Compatible with Flash 5 and later.

 

The MovieClip.unloadMovie() method unloads a movie clip.

Description

The MovieClip.unloadMovie() method is used to unload a movie that was loaded with a MovieClip.loadMovie() or MovieClip.attachMovie() method.

 

 

Code

 

 

Notes

 

 

 

myMovieClip.unloadMovie();

 

 

 

 

Unloads the movie myMovieClip.

 

 

loadMovie(“myMovieClip”);

 

 

This method does not take parameters.

 

Examples and practical uses

This method removes clips created with the MovieClip.loadMovie() and MovieClip.attachMovie() methods.

Tips and precautions

None.
