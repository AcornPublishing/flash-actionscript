MovieClip.onDragOver

myMovieClip.onDragOver

 

Where myMovieClip is a movie clip instance.

 

Compatible with Flash 6 and later.

 

The MovieClip.onDragOverevent handler is invoked when the cursor is pressed while over a movie, dragged outside the movie, and then dragged back over the movie clip’s area.

 

This event is the same as the corresponding Button event Button.onDragOver. See the entry for Button.onDragOver.
