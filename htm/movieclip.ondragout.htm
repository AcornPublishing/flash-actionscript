MovieClip.onDragOut

myMovieClip.onDragOut

 

Where myMovieClip is a movie clip instance.

 

Compatible with Flash 6 and later.

 

The MovieClip.onDragOutevent handler is invoked when the cursor is pressed and dragged outside and then over the movie clip’s area.

 

This event is the same as the corresponding Button event Button.onDragOut. See the entry for Button.onDragOut.
