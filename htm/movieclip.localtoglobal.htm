MovieClip.localToGlobal

myMovieClip.localToGlobal(coords)

 

Where myMovieClip is a movie clip instance and coords is the identifier of an object specifying the x and y coordinates.

 

Compatible with Flash 5 and later.

 

The MovieClip.localToGlobal() method converts local (movie clip) coordinates into global (stage) coordinates.  The method is the reverse of the MovieClip.globalToLocal method.  See this entry for more details of how the method functions and for examples.
