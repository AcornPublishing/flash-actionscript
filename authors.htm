  I started my mind going early in life when I was about 4 years old. At that age, I began playing the piano, which was sitting unused in our house. I’ve been playing ever since then. Later, in 1997, I co-wrote a full-length musical called Chrystanthia. Somewhere along the way, I picked up game programming as a hobby, and eventually ended up making games professionally for home console systems. Then, in 1998, I discovered how I could take all my experiences and combine them when I discovered Flash. The rest is history. I share my ideas on my website, glenrhodes.com
