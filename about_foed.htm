We at friends of ED want you to get the most out of the books that we produce. For us, the commitment to the customer doesn't just stop when you walk out of the door having bought our book, but when you finish the last page and have understood every last word to your satisfaction. Because of this, we offer much more wide-ranging support than any other publisher.

If you don't understand the concepts within the book or are having problems with a particular page or section, then email support@friendsofed.com, with the last four digits of the isbn in the subject of the mail (that's 0586 for Flash MX Designers ActionScript Reference) in the subject field of the e-mail. Your message is delivered to our crack team of support professionals who are the first people to read it. We have files on the most frequently asked questions and will answer anything generally immediately. We answer general questions about the book and CD.

Deeper queries are forwarded to the technical editor responsible for that book. They have experience with Flash MX and ActionScript and are able to answer detailed technical questions on the subject.

Finally, in the unlikely event that the editor can't answer your problem, they will forward the request to the author. We try to protect the author from any distractions from writing. However, we are quite happy to forward specific book related requests to them. All friends of ED authors help with the support on their books. They will either directly mail the customer with the answer, or will send their response to the editor who will pass it on to the reader.

If your enquiry concerns an issue that is outside of normal book support, then these type of questions are answered at the friends of ED forums.
